OUR MISSION

Prison Yoga Chicago provides trauma-informed Yoga programming as a rehabilitative tool and form of social activism for individuals impacted by the criminal legal system.

OUR VIVSION

To reduce recidivism & facilitate growth by sharing the practices of Yoga

  • Through teaching Yoga, we share tools with our students that they can use both on and off the mat including breath work, mindfulness & intention-setting
  • PYC uses educational tools both in and out of prison & jail settings to encourage self- actualization, awareness, and accountability as well as collective & social responsibility.
  • While we operate within the current legal system in order to support those who are incarcerated today, we support a reformed vision for correctional facilities- one centered around rehabilitation, safety, and equitable treatment for all.
